Frequently asked questions

Are tours suitable for children or seniors?

Yes — tell us your group size and pace; we often recommend 2–3 hour private trips for a more relaxed rhythm.

When is the best time for snorkeling?

Tides and weather change through the year. See our Facebook page for the current best dates.

Do I need to pay a deposit?

Chat on WhatsApp — we explain deposit or full payment depending on the package and peak dates.

Do you provide hotel pickup?

Some packages include pickup; others meet at Tanjung Rhu jetty. We confirm transfers on WhatsApp before booking.
